A Pong Game in Assembly Language

Abstract

This project is an implementation of a simplified version of a classic game called Pong in VHDL, and make it playable on GECKO4Education. Briefly, the game amounts to the following. A ball is moving around the playfield diagonally, at a constant speed, bouncing off the walls and the bat controlled by the player using two buttons. The bat can move only vertically. Each time the ball hits the left wall, the players score gets increased. Each time it hits the right wall, the opponent wins a point. Players wins by reaching the score of 9 before his opponent, otherwise they lose. Hence, their goal is to use the bat to guard the right wall as well as possible.
